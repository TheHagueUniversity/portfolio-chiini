About Me

My name is Hua "Chiini" Lin and I am 21 years old. Born in China and grew up in Austria – the best influences of both worlds! Since I grew up with those two vastly different cultures, I learned to adapt very early on in life. This made me very flexible and adaptable to others when it comes to communication and teamwork. In general I can be described as a very ambitious person, I moved to the Netherlands without hesitation to pursue my studies in User Experience Design.

My greatest passion is being creative - in all forms! From painting to embroidery to design! Expressing myself through my craft is my favorite thing to do. The goal is to always astound the users/clients with innovative design visions.

My Soft Skills include being creative, team work, organising, empathetic, leadership and intercultural communication.

My Hard Skills are my knowledge in Microsoft Office, the Adobe programs, JavaScript, HTML5, CSS, C# and Processing. I also posess the C2 language skills in both English and German with Mandarin being my mothertongue.
