Paranoia

This is my Graduation Project in highschool in form of a game, where the mental disorder Paranoia is being shown!

Paranoia is a Jump'n'Run game, my project team and I did from scratch with me as the team leader. It is a serious game, where the struggles and phases of the mental disorder Paranoia is being shown. The game has a storyline of a young boy Milo, who developes Paranoia. The story is portrayed in cutscenes and the user has to clear levels to get to the next cutscene. The goal is to give the user information about Paranoia.

I was responsible for the Game Design, User Experience Design, User Interface Design, Illustrations and Programming while my teammembers did the 3D Modelling/Animation and Audio/Sound Production.

The programs that have been used are: Autodesk Maya - 3D Modelling/Animation, Steinberg Cubase - Audio/Sound Production, Unity - Programming/Rendering in C#

We participated in the youth innovative competition for students in Austria called "Jugend Innovativ" and made it into the Semi-Finals.
